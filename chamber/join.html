NP Membership Benefits

Designed for non-profit organizations. Includes a standard directory listing and access to community networking events at no cost.

Bronze Membership Benefits

Includes a standard directory listing, access to general meetings, training workshops, and member-only discounts.

Silver Membership Benefits

Includes all Bronze benefits plus homepage banner rotation, two free event tickets per quarter, and social media promotion.

Gold Membership Benefits

Includes all Silver benefits plus primary spotlight positioning, event sponsorships, annual luncheon speaking opportunities, and dedicated marketing emails.
