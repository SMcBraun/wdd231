About Me

My name is Sina McBraun, and I am currently pursuing my second degree through BYU–Idaho's Software Development program. As a nontraditional student, software development was never something I imagined pursuing earlier in life. Throughout my career, I have worked in fast-paced environments where I learned to adapt quickly, analyze complex situations, and help improve processes so work could run more smoothly. Those experiences inspired me to continue my education and strengthen technical skills that challenge me to grow. My goal is to expand my knowledge, build a strong foundation in software development, and create new opportunities for both my professional career and personal growth.
